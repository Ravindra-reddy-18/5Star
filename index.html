King of Kentucky

King of Kentucky – Rare Bourbon Whiskey A true prize for bourbon collectors, King of Kentucky stands among the most coveted limited releases in American whiskey. Crafted by Brown-Forman, this single-barrel bourbon is known for its rich character, exceptional balance, and remarkable depth.
Our collection features all three batch releases, each aged 12 years and bottled at varying proofs—105, 107.5, and 110—offering a unique tasting journey across the lineup. Expect bold notes of caramel, oak, dark fruit, and warm spice, layered with a smooth, full-bodied finish that reflects its meticulous aging process.
Extremely limited and highly sought after, King of Kentucky is more than a bourbon—it’s a statement piece for any serious whiskey enthusiast.

Buffalo Trace - Kentucky Straight Bourbon Whisky

Crafted at the legendary Buffalo Trace Distillery, Buffalo Trace is a timeless bourbon that delivers exceptional quality and consistency in every pour. Known for its smooth, approachable profile, it’s a staple for both seasoned whiskey drinkers and those just getting into bourbon.
This award-winning bourbon features inviting notes of vanilla, caramel, and toffee, balanced with hints of spice, oak, and a touch of mint. The finish is long, smooth, and slightly sweet—making it perfect for sipping neat, on the rocks, or in classic cocktails.
Reliable, refined, and endlessly enjoyable, Buffalo Trace represents the heart of Kentucky bourbon tradition

Limited Releases – Penelope Collection

Penelope’s limited releases are where craftsmanship meets creativity. These rare selections highlight some of the most unique barrels, showcasing exceptional blending and innovative finishing techniques.
From bold, experimental profiles to carefully aged expressions, each bottle is designed to stand out and deliver a memorable experience for both enthusiasts and collectors.
