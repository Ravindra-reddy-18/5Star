About Us

Five Star Liquor is Crown Point's neighborhood shop for rare bourbon, allocated whiskey, everyday favorites, and real personal service.

Established in 2021, Five Star Liquor was built with a simple goal: offer an exceptional selection of high-quality alcoholic beverages in a store that feels welcoming, well-organized, and easy to shop. From rare bourbon and allocated whiskey to craft beer, tequila, champagne, wine, and trusted everyday staples, we work hard to keep a lineup that serves both collectors and casual shoppers.

We are committed to pairing that selection with attentive, knowledgeable customer service that meets the needs of our diverse community. Whether you are celebrating, stocking up for the weekend, searching for a special bottle, or just need a quick recommendation, our team is here to help you find the right fit without the guesswork.

Conveniently located right off I-65 in Crown Point, we are proud to be a dependable local stop for customers who value quality, variety, and personal service. We want every visit to feel straightforward, helpful, and worth coming back for.
